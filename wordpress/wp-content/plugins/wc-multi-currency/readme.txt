WC Multicurrency is a prominent currency switcher plugin for WooCommerce. This plugin allows your website or online store visitors to switch to their preferred currency or their country's currency. WC Multicurrency has the option to select currencies that you want to enable on your online store. Currency rates can be set automatically or manually.
